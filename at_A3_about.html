Customer Service Skills

Customer service is essential for a training academy because students and café partners need reliable, respectful and friendly support.

Evidence of Skill

  • Welcoming customers in a café environment.
  • Listening carefully to customer needs and responding politely.
  • Handling busy service periods while staying calm.
  • Explaining products and services clearly.

Example Workplace Scenario

If a student is confused about a course booking, I would listen first, confirm the details, explain the next step and follow up professionally. This shows patience, empathy and problem solving.

Value to the Company

Good customer service improves trust, repeat enrolments and the reputation of BrewMaster Barista Academy.
